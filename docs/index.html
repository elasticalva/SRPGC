RPG it All

SRPGC its first an Role Playing Game, where you can customize your character from a pool of heroes with upgradeable items and habilities, that you earn in a form of cards while you progress

Play your Cards

Your set of cards will define how your character look, what are his habilities and what level threat is. Your collection or deck of cards improve as you fight enemies Documentation directory.

Fight in PVP

Defeat other players to steal his cards in order to improve your deck, items and habilities. If you get defeated you loose part of your cards.
